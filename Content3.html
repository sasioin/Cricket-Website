FAMOUS CRICKETERS

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He is considered one of the best batsmen in the world and has numerous records to his name.

Kane Williamson

Kane Williamson is a New Zealand cricketer and current captain of the New Zealand national team. He is known for his technical batting and exceptional leadership skills.

Steve Smith

Steve Smith is an Australian cricketer and former captain of the Australian national team. He is known for his unorthodox batting style and has been described as one of the best Test batsmen in the world.

Babar Azam

Babar Azam is a Pakistani cricketer and current captain of the Pakistan national team. He is considered one of the best batsmen in the world and has broken numerous records in his career.
